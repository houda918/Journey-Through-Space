Learn about the planets in our solar system

Mercury

The smallest and closest planet to the Sun.

Venus

Known for its thick atmosphere and high temperatures.

Earth

Only known planet with life and liquid water.

Mars

The "Red Planet," has the tallest volcano in the solar system (Olympus Mons).

Jupiter

Largest planet, gas giant, has a Great Red Spot (massive storm), over 90 moons.

Saturn

Known for its beautiful rings, gas giant, also has many moons.

Uranus

Ice giant, rotates on its side, pale blue due to methane.

Neptune

Farthest from the Sun, strong winds, deep blue color, also an ice giant.
